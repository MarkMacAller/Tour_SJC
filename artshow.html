The St James Court Art Show

The Art Show began in 1957 as a vehicle to raise funds for recent repairs to the historic fountain on the court. Early exhibitors attached artwork to close lines strung between the court's oak trees. The Art Show is now an annual event and draws more than 700 artist from around the country.
